Types of Chickens

the Silkie chicken is a fluffy, adorable chicken that originates from China. They are known to have calm, friendly personalities, making them adorable pets

the Orpington chicken is a chicken native to Britain. It is known to have a calm personality, and comes in a wide range of colours, including blue and black

the Araucana is a species of chicken native to Chile. They are calm, and the eggs they lay come in a beautiful, stunning shade of blue.

the Ayem Cemani is a chicken from Indonesia. It's striking appearance, drenched in a singular colour, makes it an eye-catching type of chicken.

the Ga Noi is a chicken known for its exceptional fighting skills. It has a fiery and angry temperament, and because of this, many people use it in chicken fights

the Jersey Giant is a large chicken known for being the largest and heaviest chicken breed. It was created in the 1800s in New Jersey, hence the name
